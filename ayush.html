ANOMS

Accounts Note Online Monitoring System (ANOMS) is a Web-based application for online Addition, Modification, Status Updation and Customized Reports generation. In addition, more than a dozen standard reports are available for convenient & effective monitoring.

The application has been designed & developed in-house by IT Centre, S.E.Railway, Garden Reach. The programme has been developed by using Oracle 11g as back-end and asp.net as front-end user interface. The application is hosted in the server at IT Centre/GRC.

ANOMS is accessible to various stakeholders through User-Specific secured Login Authentication over Railnet by any Railnet user.

Notification

The ISAs and SVs are eyes and ears of Accounts department as they are entrusted with the challenging job of verifying huge stock spread over length and breadth of a Zonal Railway to check over-stocking & ensure that there is no leakage of Railway Revenue. Stock verification is the important tool for Inventory Control:

Objective:

  1. Ensure correct Receipt and Issue of materials either Stock or Non-Stock.
  2. Taking unaccounted items into books of record to eliminate the scope of misappropriation.
  3. Monitoring the disposal of Stock Verification report so that proper action may be resorted against defaulting staff in time to arrest the chance of losses of revenue.
